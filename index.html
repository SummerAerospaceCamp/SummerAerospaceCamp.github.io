Aerospace Engineering Project

UT Arlington Summer Camp Aerospace Engineering Project

Project Description

Pine Garden City was hit by a devastating earthquake of magnitude 7.2
Because of that, the whole city was evacuated and transferred somewhere else for safety reasons. However, 5 people were found missing.
As an aerospace engineer, the Government of the United States asked you to build a latest generation drone, to find the missing inhabitants in Pine Garden City.
To complete your task, you will need:
- A drone Pilot
- A Navigator (who possesses the city map).
- A Government spokesperson that notifies the Government once an inhabitant was found and keeps a list of the rescued people
Each team will first setup the virtual city (in Unity3D), using the tutorial provided by the Instructor. Then, you may start the rescue operation, and try to find all the missing inhabitant in Pine Garden City.
Once the missing people are found, the Government spokesperson will be asked to give a report, giving the details of the rescue operation, where and when each inhabitant was found and each teammateâ€™s participation.
